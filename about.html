A Passion for Broadcasting

Kanishka Meegasmulla is one of Sri Lanka's most dynamic and recognizable television personalities. With a career rooted in a deep passion for communication and entertainment, Kanishka has carved a unique space for himself at Hiru TV.

From breaking news updates to hosting primetime entertainment shows, his versatility makes him a true icon of modern broadcasting. His charismatic presence on screen, combined with his unparalleled ability to connect with audiences, has garnered him a massive following across the island.

Career Highlights

  • Lead Anchor for major live events and award ceremonies.
  • Pioneer of high-energy morning/evening lifestyle shows on Hiru TV.
  • Award-winning engagement with live studio audiences.
  • Extensive experience in interviewing high-profile personalities, celebrities, and thought leaders.

The Vision

"For me, television is not just about being in front of a camera. It's about being invited into people's living rooms, into their lives. It's a privilege I don't take lightly. My goal is to entertain, inform, and inspire, ensuring every second the audience spends with me is memorable."
